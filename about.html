About AQUALIS

Gaze beyond the surface

Who We Are

AQUALIS is an online journalism platform committed to exploring the stories that lie beyond the headlines. We believe the ocean is more than just a resource; it's a living, breathing part of our identity and our future. This is the platform where you can slow down, look deeper, and uncover the extraordinary stories happening in our blue world.

Our Mission

Ever got tired of scrolling through headlines that only told half the story? At AQUALIS, journalism is no longer about being first; it's about being fair and insightful. We started AQUALIS because our mission is to question the usual narratives, give space to underrepresented voices, and provide context so readers can see the full picture of our relationship with the sea.

Join Our Journey

At AQUALIS, we are constantly seeking new perspectives, collaborations, and curious minds who share our vision of thoughtful journalism. If you have stories that matter or ideas that spark change, we invite you to join us as contributors or partners in our quest to keep the ocean's stories deep and clear.
